Template Settings
This template has the following variables:

Main Usage:
{part} to access the selected part of a record.

Part Context:
{part} to get the part of the selected record and field.

The part consists of three main elements:
{part.field} to get the field instance.
{part.recordValue} to get the recordValue instance.
{part.fieldvalue} to get the fieldvalue.

To obtain the final value, use {part.value}

Other Variables:
{cObj} to get information about the current content object.
